Our Cavity Wall Insulation Services

EcoVert Academy connects you with the best experts and solutions for optimal thermal insulation of your buildings.

EPS Injection Insulation

An economical and efficient solution, injecting expanded polystyrene (EPS) beads into cavity walls ensures superior thermal and acoustic insulation, significantly reducing your energy bills.

Polyurethane Foam Injection

For maximum performance, injected polyurethane foam offers insulation without thermal bridges, perfect air sealing, and exceptional durability, ideal for demanding projects.

Detailed Thermal Audits

Our experts conduct precise diagnostics of your building's energy performance, identifying heat loss points and proposing customized insulation solutions.

Humidity Control and Ventilation

Insulation goes hand in hand with good humidity management. We offer integrated solutions to prevent condensation and ensure healthy indoor air quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is cavity wall insulation?

It's a technique that involves injecting insulating material (such as EPS beads or polyurethane foam) into the empty space between two exterior walls, improving their thermal performance.

How long does installation take?

Duration depends on the building's size and complexity, but most residential installations are completed in a single day, with minimal disruption.

What materials are used?

We focus on injecting expanded polystyrene (EPS) beads and polyurethane foam, known for their high thermal performance and durability.

Is the insulation compliant with Moroccan standards?

Absolutely. All installers certified by EcoVert Academy strictly comply with Law 47-09 on energy efficiency and thermal building regulations (RTCM) in force in Morocco.

Are there aids or subsidies available?

Morocco encourages energy efficiency. Our experts can inform you about potential government programs or tax incentives available for insulation.

What is the warranty on the work?

Work performed by our certified installers benefits from a ten-year warranty, ensuring the durability and performance of your insulation.
